About

Who am I?

Hi There! I'm Jack Yang. I'm a mission-driven Virtual Reality developer with a focus on Virtual Experience and Simulation.

I am passionate about building products that can change how people perceive the world. My background in communication helps me to facilitate better teamwork and user experience.

I also enjoy building mobile apps for fun because I see it as a playground for my ideas and UI designs

Kitchen Kraving

A Chef-theme Virtual Reality game

This is a virtual reality game is developed during the summer of 2015 at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) Entertainment Technology Center (ETC). The setting is in a kitchen where the player is a hungry chef that is trying to steal food to stay alive while not get caught by the randomly checking-in boss. At the same time, the player has to cook enough food to score more chef points.
